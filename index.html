Where Can Rethink Food Deliver Its Meals On Time?

More than 180,000 asylum seekers have arrived in New York City in the last 18 months.

Rethink Food is a nonprofit that delivers meals from restaurants to asylum seeker shelters.

This tool can help Rethink Food plan its deliveries and identify where in the city it should seek out new restaurant partners.

Click a blue pin to learn about the restaurant and see how far food cooked at that location can travel in 30 minutes of driving.
